Approach

Quiet process, disciplined validation.

Data security

Confidentiality is operational.

We minimize data collection, compartmentalize access, and use encryption as a baseline. Corporate files are hosted on an encrypted server environment managed through Proton Business services in Switzerland. We respond to lawful requests for information consistent with what is required and what is permitted under applicable Swiss privacy laws, and we encourage clients to consult counsel for jurisdiction specific legal guidance.
